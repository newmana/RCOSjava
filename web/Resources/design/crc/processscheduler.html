ProcessScheduler CRC

Name:

 ProcessScheduler

Comment:

 Responsible for managing process creation, termination and scheduling. Handles messages such as fork process, exec process, kill process, schedule new process, block process etc. Maintains all disk queues and includes methods such as runningToReady, runningToBlocked, getNextRunningProcess.

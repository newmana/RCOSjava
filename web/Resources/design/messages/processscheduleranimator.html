/ Resources / Design / Messages / ProcessSchedulerAnimator


This currently has fairly limited interaction by a user. They can modify the Quantum and the speed at which the animation runs. It also allows a person to click on the CPU to view its status.

By taking all messages from all the various components of the OS it illustrates what the current processes are doing.
